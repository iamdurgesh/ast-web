What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function, remember your preferences and, where you consent, help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

How we use cookies

We use two categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to work, including remembering your cookie preferences. These are always active and do not require consent.
  • Optional analytics cookies — help us measure how visitors use the site so we can improve the experience. These are only set once you give your consent, and no non-essential cookies load before then.

Managing your preferences

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, though some site features may not work as intended if you do.

Third-party cookies

Where we use optional analytics, some cookies may be set by trusted third-party providers. These are only ever loaded after you have given consent. This template does not set any third-party cookies by default.
